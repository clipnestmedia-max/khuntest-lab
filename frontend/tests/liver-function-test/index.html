What is the Liver Function Test?

A liver function test checks markers that help doctors assess liver health, bile flow and protein balance.

Why is it done?

  • Evaluation of jaundice symptoms.
  • Monitoring SGOT and SGPT liver enzymes.
  • Routine health checkup or medication monitoring.

Preparation

Fasting may be advised depending on the exact profile or related tests.
