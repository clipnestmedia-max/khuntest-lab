What is the Kidney Function Test?

A kidney function test measures blood markers that help doctors assess kidney filtration and electrolyte balance.

Why is it done?

  • Monitoring creatinine and urea levels.
  • Screening kidney health in diabetes, blood pressure or routine checkups.
  • Reviewing electrolyte status when advised by a doctor.

Preparation

Share medicine details with the lab. Fasting may be advised if KFT is combined with other tests.
